About Me

I'm Earl John, a Filipino full-stack developer who likes to create animated and responsive websites. I had held a position as a delivery rider for one of the major online retailers in the Philippines, but I left to seek a career in the IT sector. I've performed numerous remote freelancing projects, especially as an encoder, but that's not what I want to do.Back in college, I had a fondness for coding, so I made the decision to study whenever I had free time while working as a freelancer and encoder. I've read and seen a lot of tutorials about web design and development, and as I was surfing Facebook, I spotted adverts for Zuit, one of the best coding bootcamps in the Philippines. I made an attempt to apply, and I was granted admission as a student. It assists me in improving my coding abilities and becoming an effective full-stack developer.
